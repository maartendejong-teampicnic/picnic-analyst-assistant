# Picnic Business Context

## Company & Markets
Picnic Technologies — online grocery delivery operating in NL, DE, FR.
- **NL**: primary market, largest volume
- **DE**: second market
- **FR**: smallest market — may need longer A/B test duration

## Core Vocabulary
- **FC**: Fulfilment Center (warehouse)
- **Hub**: local distribution depot, closer to the customer
- **Article**: product / SKU
- **Selling Unit (SU)**: purchasable item (can bundle multiple articles)
- **key_***: surrogate keys generated by the DWH team (e.g., `key_order`, `key_customer`)
- **Braze**: CRM / marketing automation platform
- **promo_calendar_week**: commercial week — use instead of ISO week in all reports
- **PEE_MARKET**: dbt env variable for market-gating logic

## Key Metrics
- **IPD**: Items Per Delivery
- **GMD**: Gross Margin per Delivery (requires `analyst_finance` role)
- **GMI**: Gross Margin per Item
- **WoW**: Week-over-Week
- **CW**: Calendar Week (use `promo_calendar_week` / `dm_date` not ISO weeks)

## Customer Segmentation
- **Active**: placed ≥1 order recently (defined by targeting model)
- **Mature**: been a customer long enough to be stable (not new)
- **Active + Mature**: standard A/B test audience
- Activity tiers: Active / Passive / Churned (per L3 category in targeting model)

## Analytical Roles in Snowflake
- `ANALYST`: standard analyst role — access to dim + edge models
- `analyst_finance`: extended role — adds access to `ft_article_profit_loss` (GMI/GMD)
  Add `USE ROLE analyst_finance;` at top of queries that need GMD.
- `USE SECONDARY ROLES ALL;` — fallback if permissions error on specific tables

## Writing Conventions
- Use Picnic week references: "CW12 2025" not "March 24–28"
- Reference markets explicitly: "(NL only)", "(NL/DE)", "(all markets)"
- Quantify impact: "8% improvement" or "€50K revenue impact" — not "significantly improved"
- Use `dm_date.promo_calendar_week` format (YYYYWW) in technical references
